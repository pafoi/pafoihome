# Ideas & Projects

This is where we collect the **seeds** and **plants** of the PaFoI forest.

- **Seeds** are small ideas, questions, wishes, and sparks.  
- **Plants** are experiments, projects, and gatherings growing from those seeds.

You don’t have to have a full project plan. A single sentence is enough to start.

---

## Seeds – Idea Cards

Seeds often begin as quiet thoughts:

- “I wish there was a space where people like me could meet.”  
- “I want to learn something new, but I don’t know where to begin.”  
- “I see something in my neighbourhood that bothers me, and I’d like to explore it.”

At PaFoI, we turn these into **Idea Cards** – short notes that say:

- What you care about.  
- Why it matters (to you or others).  
- What a tiny next step might be.

You can:

- Write an Idea Card just for yourself.  
- Share a seed so others can see it, encourage you, or join in.  

[Share an Idea Seed](/start#stuck) <!-- later link to a form -->

## Plants – Experiments and Projects

Some seeds want to grow.

A **Plant** can be:

- A small online circle that meets for a few weeks.  
- A one‑off gathering or workshop.  
- A simple experiment in your home, street, school, or community.  
- A prototype for a longer‑term project or venture.

We use **Project Notes** to keep things light but clear:

- What we are trying.  
- Who is involved.  
- How long we’re trying it for.  
- How we’ll know what we learned.

You don’t have to do this alone. Companions and other participants can help you clarify and tend your plant.
