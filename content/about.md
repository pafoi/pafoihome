# About Plant a Forest of Ideas

Plant a Forest of Ideas (PaFoI) is a gentle movement and practice for people who want to live, learn, and thrive more meaningfully – especially when life feels stuck, changing, or too fast.

We believe that everyone carries seeds of ideas, questions, and possibilities. When those seeds are seen, named, and tended together, they can grow into projects, communities, and ways of living that help all of us flourish.

---

## Why PaFoI exists

Many of us feel:

- Underused or invisible after retirement or life changes.  
- Unsure what to do next after a job ends or a career feels “done”.  
- Exhausted by care work, with little space for our own interests.  
- Lost in studies or early work, wondering what really matters.  

PaFoI exists to offer a soft, welcoming field where you can:

- Notice and name what you care about.  
- Start with very small steps, without pressure to have everything figured out.  
- Learn by developing ideas and trying gentle experiments with others.  

You don’t have to arrive confident, productive, or “ready”. You can come as you are.
