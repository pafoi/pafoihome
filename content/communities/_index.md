# Communities & Events

PaFoI is not just a website or a set of tools. It is made of **people meeting each other**, gently and regularly, in circles and gatherings.

This page shows you the kinds of spaces we hold and how you can join in.

---

## Welcome to the Forest sessions

If you are new, this is the best place to start.

**Welcome to the Forest** sessions are:

- Short, friendly introductions to PaFoI.  
- Spaces where you can listen, ask questions, and meet others.  
- Hosted online, with occasional in‑person versions.

In each session we:

- Share the Seed–Plant–Forest metaphor.  
- Explain the different circles and how they work.  
- Invite you to notice what resonates for you right now.
