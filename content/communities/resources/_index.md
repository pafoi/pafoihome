# Resources

This is the **library** of the forest.

Here you’ll find simple guides, real stories, and seasonal reflections to help you:

- Start when you feel stuck.  
- Host small circles and gatherings.  
- Turn ideas into gentle experiments.  
- Learn from what others have tried.

You don’t need to read everything. Just pick what feels useful right now.

---

## Getting started guides

These are short, practical guides you can use on your own or with a friend.

### Starting when you feel stuck

For times when you feel bored, low, or unsure what you want.

This guide offers:

- A few very small reflective questions.  
- A way to notice what matters to you without pressure.  
- A simple way to turn one feeling into an Idea Seed.

[Read “Starting when you feel stuck”](#) <!-- link to PDF or Notion later -->

### Turning an idea into a tiny experiment

For when you have a spark and want to try something small.

This guide walks you through:

- Clarifying what you’re curious about.  
- Choosing a small, time‑bound experiment.  
- Noticing what you learn without judging yourself.

[Read “From idea to tiny experiment”](#)

### Hosting a small circle

For anyone who wants to gather 3–8 people to talk, listen, and explore together.

You’ll find:

- A simple structure for 60–90 minutes.  
- Sample prompts and check‑ins.  
- Tips for making it feel safe and welcoming.

[Read “Hosting a small circle”](#)

## Forest Reports

At the end of each season and year, we publish **Forest Reports**.

They include:

- Seeds: themes and questions people are exploring.  
- Plants: projects and experiments that ran.  
- Forest: changes in communities, relationships, and partnerships.  
- Patterns: what seems to help people feel more alive, connected, and hopeful.

These reports are written in plain language so that:

- Participants can see themselves and their impact.  
- Partners (schools, communities, organisations) can learn from what’s emerging.  
- Others can adapt and build on the ideas.

We’ll list them here as they become available:

- Forest Report – Season 1 (coming soon)  
- Forest Report – Season 2 (coming later)  
- Annual Forest Publication – Year 1 (coming later)
